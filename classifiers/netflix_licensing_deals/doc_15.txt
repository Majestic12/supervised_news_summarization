Netflix orders second season of 'Daredevil'.
Be careful, bad guys. Daredevil will be around to fight injustice for another season.
Netflix on Tuesday confirmed it had picked up a second season of Marvel's Daredevil that will premiere in 2016. The streaming service made the 13-episode first season available starting April 10.
Daredevil centers on Matt Murdock (Charlie Cox), who was blinded as a young boy but possesses extraordinary senses. He fights against injustice by day as a lawyer, and by night as superhero Daredevil in modern-day Hell's Kitchen in New York.The dark drama also stars Rosario Dawson, Vincent D'Onofrio, Deborah Ann Woll, Elden Henson, Scott Glenn, Bob Gunton, Toby Leonard Moore, Vondie Curtis Hall and Ayelet Zurer.
Doug Petrie (Buffy the Vampire Slayer, American Horror Story) and Marco Ramirez (Sons of Anarchy) will oversee the show's operation during Season 2.
