Statue of Liberty given all clear after reports of a suspicious package.
The New York City Police Dept. bomb squad was at the Statue of Liberty investigating reports of a suspicious package. Visitors were evacuated off Liberty Island.
Officials gave an all clear about 4 hours after evacuations began. The National Park Service said April 24 that all visitors and personnel on Liberty Island were safe. The Service said in a statement that someone placed a 911 call around 11am saying they were going to "blow up" the Statue of Liberty.
