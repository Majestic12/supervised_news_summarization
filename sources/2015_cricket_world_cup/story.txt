Australia wins 5th Cricket World Cup with victory over New Zealand.
The 2015 ICC Cricket World Cup began Feb. 14 and was hosted by Australia and New Zealand.
Australia won the Cricket World Cup with a victory over New Zealand. The Melbourne Cricket Ground had a record crowd of 93,013. Australia beat India in the semifinals, while New Zealand beat South Africa to make its first appearance in the finals. Australia previously won in 1987, 1999, 2003 and 2007.
Afghanistan defeated Scotland by a single wicket, its first ever Cricket World Cup win, in Dunedin, New Zealand on Feb. 24. The final score was: Afghanistan 211-9, Scotland 210 (all out).
